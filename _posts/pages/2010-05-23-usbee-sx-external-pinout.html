The pinout of the 11 pin header according to the USBee ZX toolbuilder document:

PinColorDescription
Pin 1 BlackData In/Out Bit 0
Pin 2 BrownData In/Out Bit 1
Pin 3 RedData In/Out Bit 2
Pin 4 OrangeData In/Out Bit 3
Pin 5 YellowData In/Out Bit 4
Pin 6 GreenData In/Out Bit 5
Pin 7 BlueData In/Out Bit 6
Pin 8 PurpleData In/Out Bit 7
Pin 9 WhiteRead/Write# Output for bit-bang mode
Trigger for signal generator mode (R/W#/TRG)
Pin 10 GreyClock Output (CLK)
Pin 11 BlackGround
